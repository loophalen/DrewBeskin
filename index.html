DREW BESKIN

The new album Problematic for the People will be released July 2, 2021

Drew Beskin, a seasoned songwriter based in Athens, Georgia, brings his nuanced approach to indie pop rock from over a decade of releasing albums as a solo artist and frontman for The District Attorneys, Party Dolls and PURSES.

Beskin's upcoming third solo LP is due in the Summer of 2021. The album was recorded/produced by Drew Vandenberg (Faye Webster, of Montreal, Kishi Bashi, Tedo Stone) at Chase Park Transductions and Matt Martin (of Wanderwild ) at Gift Horse Studios both located in Athens.Album tracks "The Cut of My Jib" and "Swimming in Bed" were released late 2020 to give a taste of what to expect from the impending LP.

His band features the ultra-light talents of Phillip Brantley (of Palace Doctor and formerly Modern Skirts ) on bass, Josh Parsons (of Cowboy Curtys ) on the ole gitar, and Jeremy Wheatley (of Sailors & Ships and Crooked Fingers ) on drums. You'll find Beskin and his "Garbage Boys" frequently collaborating with many other musical gods in the Athens universe.

Beskin is Influenced heavily by Oasis, Elvis Costello, Jenny Lewis, Lindsey Buckingham, Rufus Wainwright and The Beatles and among others.

Check out more on his instagram and other social pages. Maybe find him struggling to learn TikTok @DrewBeskin
